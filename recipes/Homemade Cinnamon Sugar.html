Homemade Cinnamon Sugar

Description

In this homemade cinnamon sugar recipe, I show you how to up your cinnamon sugar game by purchasing quality ingredients. Your baked goods will be that much tastier.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup baker's ultrafine cane sugar (caster sugar)
  • 1 teaspoon ground Indonesian cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons ground Ceylon cinnamon

Steps

  1. In a glass jar with a resealable lid, add the baker's sugar and cinnamon. Mix well. Secure lid until ready to use.
